## Overview

This audit covers boutique real estate brokerages, property management firms, estate management companies, and high-end home services in the Hamptons. Focus is on independent/boutique players — not the big chains (Douglas Elliman, Sotheby's, Corcoran corporate, Compass corporate). Each entry includes a website grade, specific issues, estimated project value, and JB Andreassi connection notes.

**JB Andreassi context:** Family building company (Andreassi Development / PERT Construction), based in Southampton. Active builder in the luxury new construction and renovation market. Works throughout Southampton, Water Mill, Sag Harbor, Bridgehampton. His network almost certainly touches every category here — architects, designers, property managers all work with the same base of luxury homeowners. **Direct intro opportunity through Taylor** for most of these.
