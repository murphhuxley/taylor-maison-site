*Taylor Maison Creative — Business Development*

## Overview

15 high-priority targets across restaurants, boutiques, real estate, landscaping, event companies, wineries/marinas, and contractors. Focus: businesses that have the clientele and revenue to invest in a proper web presence but haven't done it. Ranked by opportunity score (deal size × likelihood × ease of first conversation).

**Sweet spot:** Businesses charging $500–$5,000+ per transaction that are still running on a WordPress template from 2016 or Facebook-only.
