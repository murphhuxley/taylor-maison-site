Pearl + T / TM · Monogrammed pearl

Round 4 · 2026-04-18 · eight constructions

The original pearl silhouette with the small circle replaced by a letter. Keeps the app-icon feel you liked — but the generic "highlight dot" becomes a signature, locked to your initials. Cards 1–4 use a T only (Taylor); cards 5–8 use TM / tm (full monogram). Each progresses from a literal dot-swap to a full type takeover.
