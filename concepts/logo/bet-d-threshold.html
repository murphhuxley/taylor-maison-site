Logo ideation · Bet D · Round 2

The threshold.

Maison is French for house. The mark is the portico — the architrave of the house of Taylor: two jambs, one lintel. Every client project is a threshold he builds for them; this is the doorway into his own house.

The idea: not a cartoon home. A stripped-bare architectural entablature. Reads as a gallery masthead, a shopfront, the lintel above an atelier door. Three strokes, zero ornament — the confidence of a mark that doesn't need to explain itself.
