Sunset Pearl · Top solid, bottom slatted

Round 3c · 2026-04-18 · eight constructions

Same brief in eight variations: full upper semicircle, horizontal slats in the lower half only. The top stays the pearl you already liked; the bottom becomes the Bauhaus pendant / sunset-over-horizon that makes the mark yours. First four (1–4) vary slat density. Second four (5–8) vary the structural move — flipped, extended past the disc, tapered inward, or eclipsed.

Why it works: balanced, architectural, recognizable at every size. Safe favorite.
