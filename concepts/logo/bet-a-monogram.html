The seal.

A custom TM monogram enclosed in a fine circular ring. The T's stem descends into the apex of the M's valley — one linked glyph, not two letters. Reads as a studio stamp, a coin, a signature pressed into paper. Editorial, geometric, timeless. Plays quietly next to the 3D orb without competing with it.
